Meatballs

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 4 persons
Ingredients Quantity
Minced meat 400g
Onion 1 medium sized
Breadcrumbs 2 tablespoons

Step 1:Chop onion into small pieces.

Step 2:Mix all ingredients.

Mashed potatoes

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 4 persons
Ingredients Quantity
Potatoes 1kg
Milk 3 dl
Butter 1 tablespoon

Step 1:Cook the potatoes. Peel them.

Step 2:Mash the potatoes. Add milk and butter.

Brown sauce

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 4 persons
Ingredients Quantity
Water 5 dl
Brown sauce mix 1 package
Melted butter 1 tablespoon

Step 1:Mix brown sauce mix with cold water.

Step 2:Bring it to a boil. Mix butter.
